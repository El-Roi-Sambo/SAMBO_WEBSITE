Magic System

The vast magic system will only be simplified in this website as much as possible . In the world of Empyrea, Mana is the standard source of energy for magic that many capable creatures use . It is for all beings within the reach of the Lost World.The world is a vast legacy of the creators. Mana is one of them. Mana is different yet unique, it is more mysterious to the mortal eyes than the physical laws we know of. Mana has it’s own function and flow of system different from the one commonly recognized. Up to this day scholars around the land are still confused on it’s true nature, and this is what makes it viable to our ties to this world. “Mana is everywhere, and is found anywhere.” - Juwenstall Hernnburden Mana is far from the physical laws that the naked eye can see and the mortal mind can understand, it is far greater. Transcending normal laws that explain how the world works, mana has its own laws to follow. Mana is the digital unit of life, the rival twin of physics. It is a fundamental force that supports the cycle of life. Living things all have mana, it is only irrational for a living being to have no trace of mana at all let alone be devoid. For a mortal body to have no mana means to deny life itself. There are many ways to apply this mana to such as invocation or channeling of spells. Spells are the general applications of mana and the unique way to interpret the digital world. Spells are the bridge of the physical world to interact with the digital world. Casting a spell may cost a ton of mana but it depends on your control and capacity to manipulate spells in an orderly manner. Spells are harder to learn, that is why people who have said to learn a few spells are considered gifted, opening a path for them to become mages. Everyone can use spells the only requirement for casting spells is a stable body, a mana core (to channel stronger spells), and/or existing mana in your body. Spells are used to defy the limits of the physical realm, and the only way to understand the digital world (or to be specific mana and magic) spells are used for studies by experts to further gain deeper understanding on the nature of magic. The following are the different categories of spells:

  1. Elemental- Uses the elemental foundations (or perhaps nature) to be able to channel into attacks or defense. (Such as; Light, Dark, Fire, Water, Lightning, Earth, Wind)
  2. Non-Elemental- Variety of spells that rely on other existing laws that affect the physical realm instead of the founding elements. (E.g. Gravity, Energy, Steel)
  3. Undefined- The least used category due to low efficiency and variety, however these types of spells are cost-effective. Undefined spells don’t rely on the existing physical laws, rather they rely on unknown variables that haven’t been discovered, meaning the laws used to be able to channel undefined spells are certain laws from the digital world itself. That is why undefined spells are rather mysterious and harder to manifest that most spells for they don’t have any form. (E.g. Mana Blast, Mana Barrier)
  4. Fusion- These mostly cost tons of mana to able to produce high rewarding results of powerful impacts. They are mixtures of different spells to form into one large attack/defense. These kinds of spells are highly dangerous to use, and among of the strongest spells in the world are mostly fusion spells. (E.g. Blazing Tempest, a mixture of fire and wind)

    5. There are also different levels of acquisition in achieving greater power:

    1. Burst: Able to use all types of spells of a single element without incantation. This can go without limit and only relies on mana. The casting can be continuous and multiple castings.
    2. Mastery: Complete mastery over the element unlocks the higher stages of development correlating to other elements, therefore a user can wield sub-elements that they mastered on one element that can connect to other elements. It is also possible when mastered two elements, in simpler terms the user can transition their casting from one element to another.
    3. Ascension: Although doesn't really rely or use connections between other elements. This stage of mastery enables the user to control all forms and aspects of an element that might seem impossible to unlock it's not really talking about other spell variants of an element rather other varying tiers of a single element (e.g. Origin Flame, Eternal Flame, Primordial Flame).

      4. To activate spells, one must require certain incantations specifically only for the required spells. As each level of spells get higher more mana is needed and can drain spiritual energy to a normal person who cannot handle certain pressure, only a selected few are capable to withstand these challenges. That is why some train to hone their mana control at a young age or increase their mana capacity, as when they get older it gets harder to develop your mana pool. Every spell cast a magic circle will follow, carved by symbols designated for it.

      Kingdoms

      In the written arcs of LoE. Despite the many kingdoms that exists only a few will get a spotlight as they are only relevant in the story. Most of the kingdoms are neutral or insignificant to be mentioned but they still exist to create balance and provide more soul in the world. Although there will be more kingdoms that will appear later on in the story as for right now, the story takes place on only one out of 5 continents. Such relevant kingdoms to the story are:

      • United Pythia: A vast and grand a fusion of thirty clans to form a united kingdom. One in identity. The largest nation in the continent. Yet feared due to its tyrannical past as the Grand Pythian Empire (The largest empire in the world). They are known for producing one of the highest grades of weapons and armor. The forges are what identifies them aside from war.
      • Greater Elistria: A close ally of Pythia, a technological and research focused state. They are more advance in the technology of the current era. They rival the economy of other countries. Most nations rely on the production of Goods of this country.
      • Baramian Empire: The ruling kingdom over the waters, they dominate trade through the seas. They are also the mass providers of sea mercenaries and pirates. they have vast networks that reach outside the continent
      • Kingdom of Azthea: Nation of pride, they value greatness and glory. Honor over resources and wealth. They try to surpass the top nations of the continent.
      • Polcia: The largest producer of daily necessities, mostly focusing on food and clothing. They are held high in respecf for their superior sense of fashion and arts.
      • Grand Duchy of Toka: Greatly famous for the transport. They are the center of transportation in the entire continent.
      • Almongard: Known as the Land of Warriors, produces highly talented fighters in the continent. Most world-renowed heroes and warriors came from this nation's academies. They have pride in glory in battle.
      • State of Alton: The neutral nation that strives to surive in the unfair economic balance. They are known for producing great multilingual individuals, later on becoming archeologists or translators.
      • Abra Metur: The people of the coast. They are the largest providers of many seafoods of varying types.
      • Dominion of Parxion: The second largest kingdom after Pythia, heavily recognized for their vast camps of quarries and mines. Most of the land they have now were bought, they are one the top producers of high-grade minerals
      • Orsoa: The land of swords. Respected for producing mercenaries that rival other nations with great swordsmanship training
      • Brook of Lerny: A land under pressure surrounded by great powers. Forced to obey it's neighbors the Baramian Empire, Kingdom of Azthea, and Gree. They have no pride in their military nor economy, almost in the brink of collapse.
      • Dukedom of Merbia: The nation that are great providers of wood and furniture. They are known for their great architecture over residential buildings.
      • Metrica: The center of the mercenary world. Produces mercenaries versatile to any situation their kingdom flourished due to the dependence to the talents of their mercenaries. This kingdom has a habit of sending troops in wars late.
      • Sarsiac: A nation that provides services through manpower. They have no specific talents other than providing support.
      • Gree: The largest providers of elite and top tier archers. Almost all the strongest archers came from this nation.
      • Merchant State of Xevmon: The center state of trade. Where all great merchants originate and where most merchants reside. They also provide high education of business, economics, and accountancy. Aside from business, they also give services for hosts, secretaries, analysts, and organizers.
      • Holy Monroe Empire: The Heart of the Soli Church. The origin of the Soli religion that worships the God of Light, Solin. Highly zealous of theur beliefs which can sometimes blind their own judgement.
