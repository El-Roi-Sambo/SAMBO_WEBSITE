Welcome to the Land of Empyrea!

A fictional work by El Roi Sambo (known by his pen name: Hazeleo) LOE dives deep into the classic medieval fantasy genre giving off multiple interesting traits, a magic system, and mythical creatures. The world of LOE is a huge story and widely revolves around many perspectives yet being pushed by one character. This wiki will guide you in details in the LOE lore that you may not understand but this wiki will explain!
